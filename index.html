What is HCKL?

We’re a student-first community. Our mission is to lower the barrier to tech by learning through building. HCKL operates as a non-profit and is fiscally supported by HCB.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join?

Any individual under-18 is welcome! We aim to open this space for those who are new or experienced to the field.

Is it beginner-friendly?

Yes. A guide will be provided on events in the event that a participant is completely new and the restriction of the usage of AI is lenient.

How are YSWS judged?

Originality, relevancy to the theme and how a developer showcases their project.

What does HCB support cover?

HCB acts as our fiscal sponsor to process donations, reimburse expenses, and ensure transparent accounting.
